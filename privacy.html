Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

ClownFish AI ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that provides AI-powered writing tools. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect and store:

  • Your email address
  • Your display name
  • A unique user ID (from Google/Firebase)
  • Authentication tokens (stored locally on your device)

When you use the AI tools (Writer, Humanizer, Detector, Citations, Fact Checker), the text you submit is sent to third-party AI API providers to generate a response. We do not store this text on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and verify your subscription status
  • To process one-time payments via Stripe
  • To provide AI-powered features by sending your text to AI APIs
  • To store your preferences and settings locally on your device

3. Data Storage

Your account information (email, user ID, subscription status) is stored in Google Firebase Firestore. Authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage. We do not store the text you submit to AI tools.

4. Third-Party Services

ClownFish AI uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and account storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Groq / OpenAI — AI text processing (OpenAI Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (Privacy Policy)

Text you submit to AI tools is transmitted to these providers solely to fulfill your request. We do not sell this data or use it for advertising.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history or web activity
  • We do not track keystrokes outside of the Auto Typer feature (which only types text you explicitly provide)
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect financial or payment card information (handled entirely by Stripe)

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the services described above (Firebase, AI providers, Stripe) or as required by law.

7. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

ClownFish AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
support@clownfishai.com
